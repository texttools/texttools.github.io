About

Use this tool to generate a random set of strong passwords. You can specify the length of the password, as well as the character set. For maximum security, we recommend that you keep uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols checked and only use generated passwords with "Strong" label.

You can also directly print the generated passwords by clicking "File" and then "Print". To download a copy to your computer, choose "Save as".

Password Strength Indicator

Generated passwords have indicators below them to indentify how strong it is. It can either be Strong (green), Good (orange), or Weak (red).

What Makes a Password Strong?

Complexity is the key. A strong password must be at least 8 characters long and must include at least one letter (uppercase or lowercase), at least one number, at least one special character from the following `~!@#$%^&*()_-+={}[]\|:;"'<>,.?/, and must not consists of repetitive characters.
